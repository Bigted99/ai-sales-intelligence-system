# LeadAI Product Roadmap

## Purpose

This document defines:

* What gets built next.
* Why it gets built.
* What constitutes completion.

This is a planning document.

It is NOT an architecture document.

It is NOT a requirements document.

---

# Current Stage

Status: Active Development

Current Focus:

Inbox Operations

Goal:

Allow businesses to actively manage lead conversations from LeadAI.

---

# Phase 1 — Operational Inbox

## Objective

Transform LeadAI from a lead viewer into a lead operations platform.

### Completed

* Lead Capture
* Lead Qualification
* Lead Scoring
* Lead Summaries
* Opening Message Generation
* Dashboard
* Lead Details
* Activities Timeline
* Conversation Storage
* Inbox Foundation
* Conversation Threads
* AI Sender Type
* Lead Sender Type
* Human Sender Type

### Remaining

* Realtime Conversation Updates
* Unread Message Tracking
* Human Reply System
* AI/Human Handoff

### Success Criteria

A user can:

* Receive lead conversations.
* View conversation history.
* Reply to leads.
* Track unread activity.
* Allow AI and humans to collaborate.

---

# Phase 2 — Business Knowledge Base

## Objective

Teach LeadAI how the business operates.

### Features

* Knowledge Base Module
* Document Upload
* Document Management
* FAQs
* Services
* Pricing
* Policies
* Products
* Case Studies

### Success Criteria

Businesses can upload and manage company knowledge.

---

# Phase 3 — Retrieval Layer

## Objective

Allow LeadAI to retrieve relevant business information.

### Features

* Embeddings
* Vector Storage
* Retrieval Pipeline
* Context Assembly

### Success Criteria

LeadAI can locate relevant business knowledge when answering questions.

---

# Phase 4 — AI Communication Layer

## Objective

Enable AI-assisted communication.

### Features

* Suggested Replies
* Draft Responses
* Follow-up Suggestions
* Objection Handling
* Context-Aware Responses

### Success Criteria

LeadAI can assist users with communication based on business knowledge.

---

# Phase 5 — Human Collaboration

## Objective

Create safe human oversight.

### Features

* Conversation Ownership
* Internal Notes
* Escalation Rules
* Team Collaboration
* Approval Workflows

### Success Criteria

Teams can collaborate alongside AI.



---

# Phase 6 — Channel Expansion

## Objective

Expand communication inputs and outputs.

### Potential Channels

* Email
* SMS
* WhatsApp
* Instagram
* Facebook Messenger

### Important

Channels are transports.

Channels are not the product.

LeadAI remains an AI-first lead handling platform.

---

# V1 Release Criteria

The following must be complete:

* Lead Capture
* Lead Qualification
* Lead Scoring
* Inbox
* Conversation History
* Realtime Updates
* Human Reply
* Unread System
* AI/Human Handoff
* Activities Timeline

When complete:

LeadAI becomes suitable for real customer testing and onboarding.
